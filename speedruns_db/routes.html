Routes

Designing routes isn't too different from planning a trip in real life. True, you may not plan a vacation with the sole intent to get everywhere in record time, but you still have choices available that you can map out. Routing can be as simple as playing through the game and finding out through experience what route is fastest. Other times, it takes careful evaluation of what abilities we have and what requirements we need to fulfill to complete a valid run.

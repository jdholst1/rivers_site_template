Tricks and Glitches

One of the wonders of speedrunning is the variety of categories that rise up over the years. Just about Any game has the basics of any% and 100% runs, but some games have caught too much interest to limit it to only these. Some categories arise in response to game-breaking glitches. These categories usually are made solely to exclude use of the glitch; one very well-known example of this is no-SRM(stale-reference manipulation) in OoT, since SRM trivializes most aspects of the game.
